Subsidiary company “Automotors” is a leading national supplier for the needs of Ukrainian manufacturers of mobile and stationary equipment.

We supply, provide a warranty support and after-sales service for Deutz and Yanmar engines, Allison and Eaton transmissions, MWM cogeneration plants (Caterpillar Energy Solutions GmbH), Knorr Bremse brake systems and other products of world famous brands: Meritor, AKG Thermotechnik, Hutchinson.

Engineering

OEMs and end users often require engineering services in connection with engines, transmissions or power packs. For this purpose Automotors offers the following services:
Modernization
and modification
We continuously participates in development of numerous modernization projects. In the case of engine / transmission retrofit projects we provide complex engineering support, which means design documentation and installation of a new engine and / or transmission for the equipment, which was originally designed for other unit.
Assembling
of power packs
This service includes connection of the engine and transmission and start-up of the power pack. We check all parameters by means of a testing tool in order to reach the proper powertrain operation and correct data exchange between CPUs of engine and transmission.
Developing
of CHP units
Since 2020, we have been packaging cogeneration units based on 200 kW Deutz TCG 2015 V8 gas engine.
